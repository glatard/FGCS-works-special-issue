Tristan Glatard obtained a Ph.D. in Computer-Science from the University of Nice Sophia-Antipolis in 2007. In 2008, he spent a year as a post-doctoral fellow at the University of Amsterdam, working in the Virtual-Lab for e-Science project. Since 2009, he has been a researcher at CNRS in Lyon, working on distributed systems for medical imaging. Since 2013, he is also a Visiting Scholar at the McGill University in Montreal, working in the CBRAIN team.
